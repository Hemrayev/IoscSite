What is IOSC?

“IOSC” website is the not-commercial dynamic internet website that will be used as distance subject Olympiad.
It contains 8 platforms for such subjects as physics, chemistry, math, informatics, English language, Russian language, history, biology. Moreover, this website has two separate entries for administrator and for users. The role of administrator is to manage the contests for Olympiads and to manage the website itself.
The administrator is able to modify, update and block the website, to add, delete and modify the contests; whereas, the users are the participants to the contests. Furthermore, users are allowed not only to participate to the contests; yet, they can visit and solve the previous contests that are kept in the archive of the website.
In addition, teachers can add their own tests to archive, give it to their students and see student’s results.
